# WealthOS Vision

## Mission

WealthOS is a personal wealth operating system designed to help individuals understand, manage, and grow their wealth over decades.

The goal is to provide a single source of truth for an individual's financial life.

## Core Pillars

* Net Worth Tracking
* Asset Allocation
* Retirement Planning
* Goal Tracking
* Financial Discipline
* Wealth Analytics

## Problems WealthOS Solves

Most investors use multiple platforms:

* Angel One
* CoinDCX
* Zerodha Kite
* Paytm Money
* PhonePe Gold
* ICICI Prudential

As a result, there is no unified view of wealth.

WealthOS consolidates all assets into a single dashboard.

## What WealthOS Is Not

WealthOS is not:

* A trading platform
* A stock screener
* A buy/sell recommendation engine
* Financial advice software

The purpose is awareness, planning, and wealth management.

## Long-Term Goal

To become a complete personal financial command center that can be used for decades while maintaining data integrity, transparency, and trust.
